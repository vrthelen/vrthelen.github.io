About Me

I'm about to be a 3rd year student at the University of Georgia. As a Computer Science major, I'm proficient in Java, C/C++, and (a little bit of) SwiftUI and Python.

I also happen to be pursuing the New Media Certificate, which you can read more about here!

Capybara Clicker

For my Native App Development midterm, I created a Cookie Clicker clone named Capybara Clicker. It functions exactly as the original game did - you tap on the capybara and get more capybaras in turn, buying upgrades and increasing your count exponentially.

iTunes Sampler

For my Native App Development final, I made an iTunes "sampler." The app lets you search the iTunes store for samples of songs, and lets you add them to playlists, which you can create and name.
